SOLID RAIN
What is Solid Rain?.
A hydrogel-based innovation that can absorb and store large amounts of water and release it slowly over time to support plant growth, especially in arid or drought-prone regions.
DESICCATION TOLERANT CROPS
How does it Help?
Desiccation-tolerant crops enhance food security and resilience by withstanding extreme drought conditions, making them crucial for sustainable agriculture and climate adaptation in arid regions.
NO UNWANTED IRRIGATION
Bring bright to life.
Whether it's because of droughts, floods, or just low rainfall, irrigation can be difficult to maintain. Farmers have often been forced to fall back on groundwater resources when surface water sources dry up during droughts. This is wehre We help by our concept of Solid Rain and Desciccation Tolerant Crops
